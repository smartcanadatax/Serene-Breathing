🛡️ Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Information We Collect

Serene Breathing does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account or login is required to use the app. All preferences, journal entries, meditation logs, mood tracking, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults and are never shared with us or any third party.

Personal Wellness Data

All data you enter into the app — including mood journal entries, sleep journal entries, meditation session logs, breathing history, and personal notes — is stored exclusively on your device. This data is never uploaded to any server, never shared with us, and never accessible by anyone other than you. It exists only in your device's local storage. Deleting the app permanently removes all of this data.

No Tracking or Profiling

We do not track your behaviour within the app. We do not build user profiles. We do not use cookies, device fingerprinting, or any other tracking technology. There is no analytics SDK, no crash reporting tool, and no advertising network embedded in this app.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notification permissions are requested through Apple's standard framework (UNUserNotificationCenter). All notification data remains entirely on your device and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions and any purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit 2. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment details or billing information. Apple's Privacy Policy governs all payment transactions. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID settings.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. No data is shared with or sold to third parties — ever.

GDPR (European Users)

Because we collect no personal data, process no personal data, and store nothing on our servers, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) obligations relating to data processing do not apply to our operations. All data remains under your sole control on your device.

PIPEDA (Canadian Users)

In accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), we confirm that no personal information is collected, used, or disclosed by Serene Breathing. All wellness data you record remains solely on your device.

CCPA (California Users)

We do not sell personal information. We do not collect personal information. California residents have no data to request, correct, or delete from our systems, as none exists.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no user accounts, no sign-in, and no network connectivity. If you believe a child has used the app, there is nothing to delete — no data has been collected.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store support link on the app's page.
