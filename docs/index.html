🛡️ Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Information We Collect

Serene Breathing does not collect, store, or transmit personal data for advertising, analytics, or profiling purposes. No account or login is required. All preferences, journal entries, meditation logs, mood tracking, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never shared with us.

Personal Wellness Data (On-Device)

All data you record in the app — including mood journal entries, sleep journal entries, meditation session logs, breathing history, and streak data — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data is never uploaded to any server, never shared with us, and is accessible only by you. Deleting the app permanently removes all of this data.

Apple Health Integration

With your permission, Serene Breathing may write mindful session minutes to the Apple Health app on your device. This data is stored locally by Apple's HealthKit framework and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can revoke Health access at any time in your iPhone Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices. No Health data is ever read or collected by Serene Breathing.

Gratitude Journal

Your gratitude journal entries are stored entirely on your device. They are never transmitted to any server, never read by us, and never shared with any third party — including AI services. Your private thoughts stay private. No one outside your device can access them.

AI Features & Groq API

When you use AI-powered features — including Chat with Serene, Daily Check-In insights, Mood Pattern Coach, Sleep Pattern Coach, and AI Breathing Coach — the text you submit (such as mood summaries, sleep data, and chat messages) is sent over an encrypted connection to Groq, Inc. (groq.com) solely to generate a response. This data is processed by Groq's servers and is subject to Groq's Privacy Policy. We do not log, store, or analyse these interactions on any server of our own. Your gratitude journal, meditation logs, and all other on-device data are never sent to Groq or any other AI service. If you prefer not to share any information with a third-party AI provider, these optional AI features can simply be left unused — all core app features (breathing exercises, meditation timer, sounds, mood log, sleep log, gratitude journal) work entirely on-device without any network connection.

No Tracking or Profiling

We do not track your behaviour within the app. We do not build user profiles. We do not use cookies, device fingerprinting, or any other tracking technology. There is no analytics SDK, no crash reporting tool, and no advertising network embedded in this app.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notification permissions are requested through Apple's standard framework (UNUserNotificationCenter). All notification data remains entirely on your device and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions and any purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit 2. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment details or billing information. Apple's Privacy Policy governs all payment transactions. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID settings.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. The only third-party service used is Groq, Inc., which powers the optional AI features described above. No data is ever sold to any third party.

GDPR (European Users)

Because we collect no personal data, process no personal data, and store nothing on our servers, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) obligations relating to data processing do not apply to our operations. All data remains under your sole control on your device.

PIPEDA (Canadian Users)

In accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), we confirm that no personal information is collected, used, or disclosed by Serene Breathing. All wellness data you record remains solely on your device.

CCPA (California Users)

We do not sell personal information. We do not collect personal information. California residents have no data to request, correct, or delete from our systems, as none exists.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no user accounts, no sign-in, and no network connectivity. If you believe a child has used the app, there is nothing to delete — no data has been collected.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store support link on the app's page.
