Information We Collect

Serene Breathing does not collect, store, or transmit personal data for advertising, analytics, or profiling purposes. No account or login is required. All preferences, journal entries, meditation logs, mood tracking, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never shared with us.

Personal Wellness Data (On-Device)

All data you record in the app — including mood journal entries, sleep journal entries, meditation session logs, breathing history, and streak data — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data is never uploaded to any server, never shared with us, and is accessible only by you. Deleting the app permanently removes all of this data.

Apple Health Integration

With your permission, Serene Breathing writes completed meditation sessions as Mindful Minutes to the Apple Health app on your device. This data is stored locally by Apple's HealthKit framework and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can revoke Health access at any time in iPhone Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices. No Health data is ever read by Serene Breathing — write only.

Apple Watch

Serene Breathing includes an optional Apple Watch companion app. The Watch app communicates with your iPhone using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework to sync your current breathing session and sound preferences. No data is transmitted to our servers — all communication happens locally between your iPhone and Apple Watch. The Watch app does not independently collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Gratitude Journal

Your gratitude journal entries are stored entirely on your device. They are never transmitted to any server, never read by us, and never shared with any third party — including AI services. Your private thoughts stay private.

AI Features & Groq API

The following AI-powered features send data to Groq, Inc. (groq.com) over an encrypted HTTPS connection solely to generate a response:

  • Chat with Serene — your typed messages and conversation history are sent to Groq
  • Daily Check-In — your mood rating and any notes you enter are sent to Groq
  • Mood Pattern Coach — an aggregated summary of your last 7 days of mood ratings (not raw journal entries) is sent to Groq
  • Sleep Pattern Coach — an aggregated summary of your last 7 nights of sleep data (not raw entries) is sent to Groq
  • AI Breathing Coach — the feeling or emotion text you enter is sent to Groq
  • AI Sleep Stories — the story theme you select is sent to Groq
  • Personalized Meditation — your session preferences are sent to Groq

Groq does not use your data to train its models. See Groq's Privacy Policy for full details. We do not log, store, or have access to these interactions. Your gratitude journal entries, breathing history, meditation logs, and streak data are never sent to Groq or any other external service. All non-AI features work entirely on-device with no network connection required.

No Tracking or Profiling

We do not track your behaviour within the app. There is no analytics SDK, no crash reporting tool, and no advertising network embedded in this app.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, all notification data remains entirely on your device and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit 2. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment details or billing information.

GDPR / PIPEDA / CCPA

Because we collect no personal data and store nothing on our servers, data protection obligations relating to data processing do not apply to our operations. All data remains under your sole control on your device. We do not sell personal information.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. No data has ever been collected.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.
